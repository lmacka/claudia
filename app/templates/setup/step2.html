Sign in with Google

Uses your Google account for login. The first email to complete setup binds the deploy — only that account can sign in afterwards. To rebind, an operator must clear the binding via kubectl exec. Same Google client serves Gmail/Calendar tools later if you turn them on (default off).

Google says "redirect_uri_mismatch" or "bad redirect"?

That error is from Google's side, not claudia. The OAuth client you wired into the chart needs this redirect URI listed under Authorized redirect URIs:

{{ request.url.scheme }}://{{ request.url.netloc }}/oauth/callback

Add it under Google Cloud Console → APIs & Services → Credentials → (your OAuth 2.0 Client ID) → Authorized redirect URIs, save, then retry. Also confirm the OAuth consent screen has openid, email, profile scopes added.
