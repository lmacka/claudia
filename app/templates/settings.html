Getting "redirect_uri_mismatch" or "bad redirect" from Google?

That error comes from Google, not claudia. The OAuth client in your Google Cloud Console needs this redirect URI listed under Authorized redirect URIs:

{{ request.url.scheme }}://{{ request.url.netloc }}/oauth/callback

Add it under APIs & Services → Credentials → (your OAuth 2.0 Client ID) → Authorized redirect URIs, save, then retry. The OAuth client you wired into the chart values is the one that needs this URI.
